# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Stakeholders

### Role Summary
Stakeholders define business requirements, review milestones, and provide final approval. They collaborate with project managers and business analysts to ensure alignment with business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Define and communicate business requirements
- Review and approve project milestones
- Provide feedback on deliverables
- Ensure alignment with organizational objectives

### Goals
- Ensure project outcomes meet business needs
- Provide timely approvals and feedback
- Maintain alignment with strategic goals

### Typical Communication
- Regular milestone reviews
- Feedback sessions with project teams
- Collaboration with project managers and business analysts

---

## Agile Coaches

### Role Summary
Agile Coaches facilitate agile ceremonies, support team adoption of agile practices, and help resolve workflow impediments. They work closely with project managers and team leads to ensure smooth agile processes.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate agile ceremonies (e.g., standups, retrospectives)
- Coach teams on agile principles and practices
- Identify and resolve workflow impediments
- Promote continuous improvement within teams

### Goals
- Foster a culture of agility and collaboration
- Improve team efficiency and delivery
- Ensure adherence to agile best practices

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and retrospectives
- Coaching sessions with team members
- Collaboration with project managers and team leads

---

## Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)

### Role Summary
Subject Matter Experts provide deep expertise on specific domain questions, review technical content, and ensure alignment with industry standards. They advise project teams and review deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Provide domain-specific expertise and guidance
- Review technical content and deliverables
- Ensure alignment with industry standards and best practices
- Support decision-making on complex technical issues

### Goals
- Ensure technical accuracy and quality
- Align deliverables with industry standards
- Provide timely expertise to project teams

### Typical Communication
- Ad hoc consultations with project teams
- Review sessions for technical deliverables
- Collaboration with developers and project managers

---

## Quality Assurance Leads

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads own the testing strategy, coordinate QA team actions, and validate deliverable quality. They interface with developers and project managers to ensure high-quality outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define and implement testing strategies
- Coordinate QA team activities
- Validate deliverables against quality standards
- Report and track defects and issues

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality deliverables
- Minimize defects in production
- Promote a culture of quality within the team

### Typical Communication
- Test planning and review sessions
- Collaboration with developers and project managers
- Reporting on testing progress and results
